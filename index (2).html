a thrilling conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. The film follows Earth's mightiest heroes as they embark on a daring mission to undo the catastrophic events caused by Thanos in "Avengers: Infinity War." With its epic battles, emotional depth, and satisfying resolution, "Endgame" stands as a monumental achievement in superhero cinema.

Oppenheimer

a biographical drama that delves into the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the brilliant physicist who played a pivotal role in the development of the atomic bomb during World War II. Directed by Christopher Nolan, the film offers a nuanced portrayal of Oppenheimer's complex character, moral dilemmas, and the profound impact of his work on humanity. Through captivating storytelling and powerful performances, "Oppenheimer" explores the ethical implications of scientific discovery amidst the chaos of war.


Main Casts

  • Florence Pugh
  • Emily Blunt
  • Matt Damon
  • Josh Hartnett
  • Gary Oldman
  • Devon Bostick

Director

Spiderman: No Way Home

a high-octane superhero adventure that sees Peter Parker navigating the fallout of his secret identity being exposed to the world. As he grapples with the consequences, Peter's world is further complicated when he seeks the help of Doctor Strange to erase the public's memory of his alter ego, inadvertently causing multiversal chaos. Packed with exhilarating action sequences, heartfelt moments, and unexpected twists, "No Way Home" delivers a thrilling and emotionally resonant journey for both Peter Parker and audiences alike.
